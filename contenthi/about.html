Governing Body

The Governing Body (GB) of the NWDA Society under the Chairmanship of the Secretary (Water Resources), Govt. of India, manages, administers, directs and controls the affairs and funds of the Society subject to the rules, bye-laws and orders of the Society. The GB pursues and carries out the activities of the Society as set forth in its Memorandum of Association and in doing so, follows and implements the policy directions and guidelines laid down by the Society.

Composition of Governing Body of NWDA
1 Secretary, Ministry of Water Resources, River Development & Ganga Rejuvenation, Govt. of India Chairman
2 Secretary or his nominee not below the rank of Joint Secretary, Ministry of Finance (Deptt. Of Expenditure Member
3 Secretary or his nominee not below the rank of Joint Secretary, Ministry of Finance (Deptt. Of Expenditure Member
